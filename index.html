ASME B31.8 Gas Transmission and Distribution Piping Systems

The ASME B31.8 code provides guidelines for the design, construction, inspection, and testing of gas transmission and distribution piping systems. This code ensures the safe and efficient transportation of gases like natural gas, propane, and butane.

ASME B31.1 power Piping code overview

ASME B31.1 provides requirements for the design, materials, fabrication, erection, testing, and inspection of power piping systems. These systems are generally used in electric power generation, industrial plants, geothermal heating systems, and heating plants.

ASME B31.3 process Piping code overview

ASME B31.3 applies to piping systems used in chemical, petroleum, pharmaceutical, and food processing plants. It provides comprehensive guidelines for the design, construction, and maintenance of piping systems that carry both hazardous and non-hazardous materials.

Y14.5-2018: Dimensioning and Tolerancing

ASME Y14.5-2018 establishes uniform practices for defining and interpreting dimensions and tolerances on engineering drawings. It is essential for ensuring that manufactured parts will fit together as intended, regardless of where or how they are produced.
